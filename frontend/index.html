One upload, seventeen automatic steps

Every stage runs locally with FFmpeg, OpenCV, MediaPipe, PySceneDetect and Whisper — nothing leaves your server.

Scene & silence aware

PySceneDetect finds every cut; ffmpeg's silence detector trims dead air automatically.

Face-aware reframing

MediaPipe tracks faces so auto-crop and auto-zoom keep the subject centered.

Color, sharpness & noise

Auto brightness, saturation, denoise and sharpening tuned per-clip from sampled frames.

Stabilization

Two-pass vidstab analysis smooths handheld shake without cropping too aggressively.

Loudness & music

EBU R128 loudness normalization plus an optional ducked background music mix.

Subtitles, burned in

Local Whisper transcribes speech and the SRT is hard-burned into the export.
